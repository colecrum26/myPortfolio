I've lived in Birmingham, Alabama, for over 20 years. I am an Auburn University graduate (War Eagle!) who has recently made a career switch. After graduating in December 2019, I worked in commercial banking as a Credit Analyst. While I enjoyed my work and my team, I knew it was not a career path I wanted to be on too long, so I decided to learn Web Development! I enrolled in TrueCoders in February 2023 to do just that.

Though leaving banking was a difficult decision, I knew that I wanted more freedom when it came to work-life balance, and I believe having development skills will offer that freedom. While working remotely is not necessarily a top priority, being able to access code from anywhere in the world at any time will allow me to pursue other passions that I otherwise might not be able to. For example, following a suggestion from his wife, Instagram co-founder Kevin Systrom added filters to his product at a bed and breakfast in Mexico. That story will always inspire me!
